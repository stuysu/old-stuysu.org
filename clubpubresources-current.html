One Stop Marketing

Use our One Stop Marketing Form to get the word out about your club. It connects you to the following organizations and individuals automatically.

  • Student Union - Get your event on the SU Calendar
  • Guidance Office - Get you event on the Biweekly Newsletter
  • Stuyvesant Announcements - Get your event announced during Morning Announcements
Note: Each organization independently decides whether or not to report the information you provide.

Room Reservation

All room reservation requests must be requested a week in advance. All clubs must strictly follow the Club Pub Rules, otherwise they are subject to club suspension. Room reservations are done online.

Budget

Budget Meetings are held periodically throughout the year and are limited to Clubs & Pubs. Club presidents will be notified by the SU by email when Budget Meetings are held. To request funding from the SU, you must comply with the rules in the document linked below.
