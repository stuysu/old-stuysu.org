Starting a Club?

Let us help out!

Anyone can start a club!

So you think you're ready to start a club? First thing's first. You have to know the Club Pub Rules. If you're future club breaks any of these rules, the club risks suspension.

Clubs & Pubs Rules

Club Pub Charter

All clubs must complete and submit a charter. They must be approved by the Student Union as a club, otherwise they will not be recognized as a club. Only clubs officially recognized by the Student Union can request for funding or hold meetings.
